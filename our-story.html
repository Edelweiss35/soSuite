Our Story

Jean and Simon - who are from the same town near Paris, France - connected in New York through their passion for interior design. Simon's hospitality background coupled with Jean’s financial & software experience led them to embark upon a joint entrepreneurial endeavor in Philadelphia, at the nexus between hospitality & technology.

Originally from Missouri, Patrick moved to Philly in 2014 to pursue a Master’s in Education. After graduating from Penn, he found that it was the unusual architectural charm of the city that led him to make Philly his home. In 2017, Patrick finished his last year as a teacher and began an unconventional path into real estate welcoming travelers to his new hometown through managing vacation rental properties.

Fortuitous encounters on a rooftop pool in Miami and at the restaurant Pumpkin on our beloved South Street brought the trio together. In 2018, Sosuite was born based on a shared love and affection for all things Philly.
