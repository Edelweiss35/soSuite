Guest Policy

1. No party policy

1.1 Noise - We have a strict no party policy with no excessive noise at anytime and quiet hours between 10pm and 8am.

1.2. Guests - Guests are not allowed to remain on the premises of your rented space after midnight.

2. House Rules

2.1 No smoking - We ask that our guests respect the space by never smoking, including on balconies and terraces.

2.2 Shared spaces - Some of the spaces you will be staying at have shared common spaces. Please respect your neighbors, co-tenants and clean behind you. And make some friends along the way!

2.3 Pets - While we love your furry friends as much as you do, we cannot accommodate them in our Suites. Exceptions can be made for animals that match the US Department's of Justice ADA requirements. A certificate can be requested.

3. Furniture & Objects

3.1 Don't move it - We invest in quality furniture and objects. Please don't move it around and risk damaging it and scratching the floors.

3.2 Its here to say - As of now, none of the furniture or objects can be taken with you. We have plans to allow you to objects home with you eventually - we'll keep you posted once we roll this out. Meanwhile, if you love something and want the same at home, just ask and we'd love to help!

3.2 Damages - You will not be charged a security deposit prior to your stay. However, we do keep a credit card on file in case of incidentals. In the event the space is a damaged, or our house rules violated, we will charge the amount owed to the credit card on file.
